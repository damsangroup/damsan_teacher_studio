❓ DamSan Teacher Studio — Quick Help
Record a lesson in 5 steps
1.Prepare slides: ＋ adds a board, 🖥️ adds a screen-share slide (e.g. GeoGebra). Place formulas (∑), images (🖼️) and shape diagrams before recording.
2.Check the top bar: 🎙️ Mic on, 📷 Cam if you want your face bubble, format badge (WEBM recommended).
3.Press ⏺ Record. Teach — draw, switch slides (PgUp/PgDn), work in a shared tab; recording continues in the background.
4.⏸ Pause anytime (timer stops); ⏹ Stop & Export when done.
5.The file auto-saves to Downloads (default). Preview scrubs; upload to YouTube directly.
Tools (left rail)
🖐️ Select VMove / resize / delete shapes, text, images, formulas. Drag inside to move, brass dot to resize, red ✕ to delete. Double-click a formula to edit its LaTeX.
✏️ Pen PChalk drawing. Colours & sizes in the bottom strip.
🖍️ Highlighter HTranslucent wide marker.
🧽 Eraser EWipes only your ink — paper, images and formulas underneath stay. The ring shows the exact wipe width.
🔦 Laser LFading pointer trail for "look here" moments — recorded, never permanent.
🔷 Shapes S18 shapes incl. mensuration solids (cylinder, cone, sphere, hemisphere, frustum…) drawn textbook-style with dashed hidden edges. Drag on the board to size.
🔤 Text THandwritten (Kalam) labels. On Ruled paper, text snaps onto the line. Size dropdown; default set in ⚙️.
🖼️ ImageFile picker — or drag-drop onto the board, or Ctrl+V paste a screenshot. Animated GIFs play and record.
∑ Formula MLaTeX composer with math keyboard, size slider, live preview, and a 62-formula library (Mensuration / Algebra / Trigonometry / Geometry) + ☆ your saved formulas.
Slides
SwitchClick a thumbnail, or PgUp/PgDn. Each slide keeps its own drawings, undo history, paper mode and board colour.
🖥️ Screen slideShares another tab live into the board — annotate over it; it records even while you work in that tab.
Delete✕ on the active thumbnail (confirmed).
Recording & export
Format badgeClick to switch WEBM ↔ MP4. WEBM exports get a rebuilt seek index — the timeline scrubs everywhere. Browser MP4 is streaming-style; some desktop players show no timeline.
SafetyAuto-download on stop (⚙️), 🎬 reopens the last recording, and the browser warns before closing during a recording.
In recordingsThe board, badge, cam bubble, laser and eraser ring are captured. Toolbars, slide strip and selection handles are not part of the canvas — but deselect (click empty space) before recording, as handles ARE drawn on the canvas.

If something doesn't work
Mic / cam / screen blockedBrowser permissions — works when the file is opened locally or hosted (not inside sandboxed previews).
MP4 has no timelineExpected for browser-muxed MP4 in some players — switch the badge to WEBM.
GIF not animatingFull GIF playback needs Chrome/Edge; Firefox shows the first frame.
Slides lost on refreshSlides live in memory only — prepare just before recording. Settings and ☆ My formulas do persist.
